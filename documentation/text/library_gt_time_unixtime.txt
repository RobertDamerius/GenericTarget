<h1>UNIX Time</h1>
<h3>Library</h3>
<span style="font-family: Courier New">GenericTarget / Time / UNIX Time</span>


<h3>Description</h3>
Get the current UNIX time from the system clock.


<h2>Output Ports</h2>
<h3>time</h3>
The UNIX time in seconds. The resolution is one millisecond.
<br>
<b>Data Type:</b> <span style="font-family: Courier New">double</span>
